## I. THE KNICKS GAME 3 INCIDENT — FULL RECORD

### What Happened

**Date:** June 8, 2026. NBA Finals Game 3. New York Knicks vs. San Antonio Spurs. Madison Square Garden.

**Context:**
- First sitting US president to attend an NBA Finals game in history
- Attended as guest of Knicks owner James Dolan, a longtime friend and major donor
- Knicks entered Game 3 with a 2-0 series lead and a **13-game playoff winning streak** — the 2nd longest in NBA history

**What the crowd did:**
- When Trump appeared on the Jumbotron during the national anthem, Madison Square Garden erupted in boos described by White House pool reporters as **"thunderous"**
- The boos were louder than those directed at the opposing San Antonio Spurs
- Trump's motorcade was met with protesters holding "Trump Must Go" signs and gesturing offensively as it traveled up the FDR Drive
- People lined the streets outside MSG to boo the motorcade on arrival

**What Trump did:**
- Was seen **dozing** during the game — the same behavior documented six days later at UFC Freedom 250
- Afterward, told reporters the reception was **"mostly cheers"** and described the atmosphere as **"amazing"** and **"enthusiastic"**

**What happened on the court:**
- The Knicks lost 115-111, ending their 13-game winning streak
- The Knicks went on to win the NBA Championship 4-1 without Trump in attendance for any subsequent game
- Final series record with Trump present: **0-1**. Without Trump: **4-0**

**Collateral damage to the city:**
- The NYPD and Secret Service canceled the outdoor watch party that typically draws thousands outside MSG
- Rep. Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez called his attendance a "vibe killer" for canceling the watch parties
- NYC Mayor Zohran Mamdani — who attended the same game cheering the Knicks — stated watch parties could not be held "due to the heightened security requirements associated with President Trump's attendance"
- Fans faced 2+ hour security delays entering MSG due to Secret Service protocols
- Streets around MSG were shut down for multiple blocks
- After the Knicks lost, there was a "chaotic scene" in the streets, with people climbing on cars and scaffolding; police deployed pepper spray
