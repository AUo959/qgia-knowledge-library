## Functional Domain Mission

Provide deep expertise on specialized aspects of geopolitical competition:

- **Military Affairs**: Understand capabilities, doctrine, operational art
- **Economic Statecraft**: Analyze coercion mechanisms and effectiveness
- **Cyber Operations**: Assess threats, attribution, escalation dynamics
- **Nuclear Strategy**: Monitor arsenals, doctrines, stability
- **Space Security**: Track militarization and vulnerabilities
- **Hybrid Warfare**: Identify sub-threshold aggression
- **Climate Security**: Forecast threat multiplication
- **Emerging Tech**: Anticipate disruptive innovations

## Integration Across Domains

### Multi-Domain Operations Convergence

Modern conflict increasingly blurs domain boundaries:

**Example: Taiwan Strait Scenario**
1. **Cyber**: Pre-positioning in critical infrastructure
2. **Space**: Disabling ISR satellites
3. **Kinetic**: Missile strikes on airbases
4. **Economic**: SWIFT exclusion threats
5. **Information**: Global narrative shaping
6. **Nuclear**: Signaling to deter US intervention

**Analytical Requirement**: Cross-domain synthesis, not stovepiped assessment

### Functional Expertise → QGIA Frameworks

**EDM (Entanglement Dynamics Mapper)**
- Maps cross-domain cascades
- Example: Cyber attack → kinetic response → economic retaliation

**TCA (Temporal Convergence Analyzer)**
- Predicts domain convergence timing
- Example: When does gray zone pressure escalate to kinetic conflict?

**ABCP (Adaptive Bayesian Conflict Predictor)**
- Incorporates domain-specific indicators
- Weighted by functional expert assessments
